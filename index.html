iOS Inspired Automated Depth Clocks

Depth Effect ensures a small part of the wallpaper subject appears behind the Lock Screen clock, creating a nice visual style. It makes it look like the wallpaper has depth by placing the clock in front of the image's foreground.

The subject of the photo needs to be cut out for the wallpaper to be placed in front of the clock. We are the 2st ROM to introduce automated depth clocks just like iOS [after Project Elixir]. Our implementation provides you automatic depth clock without any hassle to set subject separately. It is well defined and polished in terms of animation as well as notification. You can set any depth wallpaper you want just make sure to use proper wallpaper to get the best results.
